* Pixies are immune to pure/light. No known vulns.
* Safe area. Nice place for lvl 1-7, but be careful with the spellcasters,
* since they hurt a lot at low levels.
* If you're after the queen, I'd suggest using scroll of lightning bolt from
*   the Atlantis magic shop
